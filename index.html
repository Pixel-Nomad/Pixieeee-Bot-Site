About PixieeeeBot

PixieeeeBot is a feature-rich Discord music bot designed to deliver the best music experience for your community.

With support for multiple platforms, high-quality audio, and a wide range of commands, PixieeeeBot will keep your server entertained for hours.

  • High quality audio streaming
  • Support for YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud and more
  • Command less music search system
  • Customizable audio filters
  • Playlist support

PixieeeeBot Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Bot collects, uses, and protects your data.

2.1 Data Collected

The Bot may store the following information:

  • Server IDs – To store server-specific settings (e.g., prefix, volume).
  • User IDs – To track song requests or maintain queues.
  • Temporary Playlist Data – Only stored during active playback and deleted afterward.

The Bot does not collect:

  • Personal messages outside of commands.
  • Sensitive user information (e.g., passwords, IP addresses).

2.2 How Data is Used

  • To provide music playback functionality.
  • To improve the Bot's performance and features.
  • To enforce server-specific settings (e.g., restricted commands).

2.3 Data Retention & Deletion

  • Server and user data is retained only as long as necessary.
  • Users may request data deletion by contacting the Bot's support server.

2.4 Third-Party Services

The Bot relies on YouTube, Spotify and Soundcloud APIs for music streaming. By using the Bot, you agree to their respective terms:

  • YouTube Terms of Service
  • Spotify Terms of Service
  • Soundcloud Terms of Service

2.5 Changes to Privacy Policy

Updates will be communicated via the Bot's official support server.
